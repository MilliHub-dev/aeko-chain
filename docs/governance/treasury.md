# Treasury Management

The AEKO Treasury is a community-owned fund to support the ecosystem.

## Revenue Sources
1.  **Transaction Fees**: 50% of base fees are burned, 50% go to Validator/Treasury split.
2.  **Identity Fees**: 100% of fees for minting handles/domains.
3.  **Clearance Fees**: Fees for processing L3+ clearance applications.

## Allocation Strategy
*   **Ecosystem Grants**: 40% (Dev tools, dApps).
*   **Public Goods**: 20% (Open source libraries, education).
*   **Liquidity Mining**: 20% (Incentivizing DEX pools).
*   **Operations**: 20% (Legal, marketing, security).

## Transparency
All treasury movements are on-chain and trackable via the [Treasury Dashboard](https://treasury.aeko.chain).
